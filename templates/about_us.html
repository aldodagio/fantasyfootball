About the Application

The idea for this application began during my final semester at California State University, Long Beach. I was taking a Machine Learning class and it was taking place during the Fantasy Football season. I had been so focused on school and work, that I didn't have any time to prepare for my Fantasy Football drafts. The previous year I had made the playoffs in all three of my leagues, but this time I didn't make it in any of my leagues and even came in last in one of my leagues. This motivated me to create a tool which would logically predict what players to take in the upcoming draft, even if I didn't have time to prepare.
